---
title: "The Great White"
index: 15
---

What *power* lurks in the Great White!
Look upon that rough, stony countenance...

---

The Western man has this rite of passage.
It is—to maim, in spirit, a lesser.

---

Once, or twice, in a lifetime—is enough.

The first, a show of prowess.
The second—of *mercy*.

---

Then, he turns to cool, fossilized stone.
"Look upon my peace, and shudder!"
